About Me

About Me

  • What I Do

    I'm a Web Developer currently focused on building persuasive HTML Emails, attractive Landing Pages, and engaging Web Applications. Check out some of my work in the Portfolio section.

  • What Motivates Me

    As an individual, I am motivated by helping others. My goal is to reach a point where I can comfortably help those around me. My featured portfolio project reiterated this desire as it provides a reliable communication relay between disabled players and their larger communities. This relay allows gamers to participate in end game content that they otherwise would never be able to experience and has already helped hundreds of players.

  • My Moto: Consume Enhance Replicate

    One of the core games I was taking into account during development featured an AI that was given a directive in hopes of improving life for humanity. That directive was Consume, Enhance, Replicate. Consume knowledge, materials, and inspiration. Enhance these concepts with existing tools and associations. Replicate this process for continual growth. This has become my moto for web development.
